Telefonica

Telefónica is one of the world's largest telecommunications companies, serving millions of customers across Europe and Latin America. As part of their digital transformation journey, they needed a unified digital experience that would enhance customer engagement and streamline service delivery.

Working closely with Telefónica's digital team, we developed an intuitive and cohesive platform that simplifies complex telecommunications services. The new design system prioritizes user experience, making it easier for customers to manage their services and discover new offerings.
